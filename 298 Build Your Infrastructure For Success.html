Build Your Infrastructure For Success

infrastructure
noun
The basic physical and organizational structures and facilities needed for the
operation of a society or enterprise.

  • Definition of Infrastructure for Success : Leo defines infrastructure for the success of living a high consciousness life as the practical structures and environment we create to make living by our highest values easy, automatic, and less resistant.
  • Strategic Infrastructure Building : He ties infrastructure to strategic thinking, pointing out the necessity to build reserves and capacity—both personal and societal—to facilitate growth and development.
  • Impact of Environment on Consciousness and Development : Leo acknowledges the dependency of spirituality and a high consciousness state on the environment, stressing the hardships one might face in a negative setting and the ease in a supportive one.
  • Aligning Environment With Values : He advises identifying top values and systematically creating an environment with processes, mechanisms, and objects that make living by those values effortless.
  • Infrastructure in Personal and Societal Contexts : Leo draws parallels between personal infrastructure and societal infrastructure, citing the challenges faced by third-world countries with poor infrastructure and the societal benefits of robust infrastructures, as seen in developed countries.
  • India as an Infrastructural Anomaly : Despite poor infrastructure, India is cited as a unique case with a high concentration of spiritual masters, attributed to its rich spiritual tradition and cultural emphasis on personal development.
  • Personal Examples of Infrastructure Enhancement : Leo shares practical examples from his life, such as placing pens and post-it notes all over his home and car to capture insights quickly, highlighting the significance of addressing minor issues for larger success.
  • Investment in Capturing Insights : He discusses his extensive efforts and investments in finding the right tools to record insights—including experimenting with voice recorders and apps—demonstrating dedication to optimizing his infrastructure to align with his values and purpose.
  • Impact of optimized infrastructure on life purpose : Leo acknowledges the significant effects of optimizing his environment, such as using a voice recording app, to capture hundreds of insights related to his life purpose of sharing knowledge.
  • Alignment of infrastructure with personal values : He emphasizes how infrastructure development is intentional and closely aligned with his highest values like truth and understanding, thus facilitating the realization of his life purpose.
  • Building automated backup systems : Leo built an automated backup system with over 36 terabytes of storage to protect his digital assets, ensuring that his work towards life purpose doesn't suffer due to technical failures.
  • Systematic infrastructure development : He advocates for the systematic construction of infrastructure that directly supports one’s highest values and life purpose, as opposed to random, non-strategic development.
  • Importance of hard and soft infrastructure : Leo distinguishes between 'hard' physical infrastructure like technology and 'soft' non-material infrastructure, such as speaking ability and creative thinking, both critical for personal development.
  • Enhancement of intangible abilities : He discusses how he has worked on intangible skills like speaking and creativity, which, though challenging to develop due to their non-tangible nature, significantly boost his capacity to fulfill his life purpose.
  • Right relationships and technical skills as infrastructure : Leo considers the right relationships and technical skills, such as business and marketing abilities, to be part of one's non-material infrastructure, which is essential for achieving lofty goals.
  • Non-material infrastructure's power : He points out that while material infrastructure is useful, the non-material aspects, like mental tools and creative capacities, provide the highest increase in efficiency and results.
  • Infrastructure's role in reducing busy work : Leo suggests that well-designed infrastructure minimizes low-yield busy work, allowing individuals to focus more on living their core values without resistance.
  • Reinvestment in Personal Development : Leo advocates for strategically investing money into oneself as a straightforward method of personal development, such as buying educational resources or coaching, emphasizing that this is one of the most effective uses of money.
  • Growth Beyond Monetary Investment : While acknowledging the usefulness of financial investments in personal development, Leo underscores that the most powerful forms of growth are entirely free, hinting at a balance in approach to self-improvement.
  • Optimizing Time for Alignment with Values : Leo invites the viewer to consider inefficiencies in their weekly routines and to identify mismatches between their actions and their core values, suggesting that physical infrastructure can be developed to better align with personal objectives.
  • Lifelong Commitment to Infrastructure Building : Leo emphasizes the importance of consistent infrastructure construction, tailored to individual objectives, as a vital approach to living values effortlessly and serving one's purpose across decades, leading to powerful and fulfilling life experiences.
  • Personal Responsibility and Self-Construction : Highlighting the trap of expecting pre-existing infrastructure, Leo stresses the need for taking personal responsibility to build one's own foundation to achieve life dreams, noting spiritual or mystical expectations as a pitfall.
  • Balance Between Dreaming and Practical Building : Leo warns about becoming overly pragmatic, losing sight of one's dreams, and wrongly identifying with built infrastructure as a measure of self-worth or happiness, prompting the necessity to find a balance between idealism and pragmatism.
  • Avoiding Over-Identification with Infrastructure : Leo conveys the risk of attaching one's identity and happiness to the infrastructure one builds, advocating instead to see infrastructure as a mere tool—a bonus for personal growth, not a necessity for happiness.
  • Consistent Infrastructure Development : He urges viewers to consistently develop their infrastructure from the present time until death as a way to achieve remarkable efficiency and success, often observed in admired individuals.
  • Actionable Steps and Resources : Encouraging practical work, Leo directs viewers to a downloadable worksheet and suggests using actualized.org's forum for community support. He asserts that building the right infrastructure involves both planning and execution, with vigilance to maintain balance.
